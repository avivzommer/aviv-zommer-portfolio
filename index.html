About

Hi, I'm Aviv.

I'm a Senior Product Designer focused on complex B2B SaaS products, helping teams connect business goals, user needs, and what AI can unlock through thoughtful UX/UI. My work sits at the intersection of strategy, systems, and storytelling. With a background across brand, marketing, and product design, I bring a cross-disciplinary perspective to building products that are clear, useful, and aligned with real business value.
